# 六级作文写作框架与模板

## 图画作文框架（三段式）

### 第一段：描述图画
**模板：**
As is vividly depicted in the picture/cartoon, [描述图画内容]. The scene/image is thought-provoking and deserves our careful consideration.

**常用句型：**
- As is shown/illustrated in the picture...
- The cartoon/drawing depicts a scene where...
- From the picture, we can see that...

### 第二段：分析寓意（核心段）
**模板：**
The picture/cartoon is intended to convey the message that [核心观点]. In today's society, [展开论述]. On one hand, [正面论述]. On the other hand, [反面/补充论述]. Therefore, [小结].

**常用句型：**
- The symbolic meaning behind this picture is...
- This picture serves as a reminder that...
- The picture reflects a common phenomenon in our society...

### 第三段：表达观点/建议
**模板：**
In my opinion/From my perspective, [个人观点]. To address this issue, [建议1]. Furthermore, [建议2]. Only in this way can we [期望结果].

## 议论文框架（三段式）

### 第一段：引出话题
**模板：**
In recent years, [话题] has become a hot topic of discussion. People hold different views on this issue. Some argue that [观点A], while others maintain that [观点B].

### 第二段：论证（正反/多角度）
**模板：**
Those who support [观点A] contend that [理由1]. Moreover, [理由2]. However, critics point out that [反驳/另一观点]. They believe that [理由].

### 第三段：总结立场
**模板：**
Taking everything into consideration, I am inclined to believe that [个人立场]. [总结理由]. As long as [条件], we will be able to [美好展望].

## 高分句型积累

### 开头句
1. With the rapid development of [领域], [现象] has become increasingly prominent.
2. It is universally acknowledged that [观点].
3. The issue of [话题] has sparked widespread debate in recent years.
4. [话题] has long been a subject of controversy.

### 过渡句
1. In addition to / Furthermore / Moreover / What's more...
2. However / Nevertheless / On the contrary / By contrast...
3. As a result / Consequently / Therefore / Thus...
4. From a different perspective / On the other hand...

### 结尾句
1. In conclusion / To sum up / All things considered...
2. It is high time that we [建议].
3. Only by [方法] can we [目标].
4. I firmly believe that [展望].
